The Math

As we know the general equation for true gravitational force is

F_g = \frac{GMm}{r^2}

Since the satellite is moving in a uniform circular motion, we can use Newton's Second Law of motion and the equation of uniform circular motion to tie the two equations together and solve for the velocity of the satellite.

F_g = ma \frac{GMm}{r^2} = m\frac{v^2}{r} \frac{GM}{r^2} = \frac{v^2}{r} \frac{GM}{r} = v^2 \sqrt{\frac{GM}{r}} = v

Although we actually use the equation

\sqrt{\frac{GM}{r \green{+ h}}} = v

to allow you to set the height of the orbit
