The Math

As we know the general equation for true gravitational force is

F_g = \frac{GMm}{r^2}

Since the sattelite is moving in a centripital motion we can use Newton's Second Law of motion and the equation of centripital acceleration to tie the two equations together and solve for the velocity of the sattelite

F_g = ma \frac{GMm}{r^2} = m\frac{v^2}{r} \frac{GM}{r^2} = \frac(v^2){r} \frac{GM}{r} = v^2 \sqrt{\frac{GM}{r}} = v
