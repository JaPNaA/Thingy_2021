The Math

The magnetic force can be represeted by:

F_m = qvb

To tie the magnetic force to centripital acceleration we can use Newton's Second Law to tie the equation of the magnetic force and centripital acceleration to find the velocity of the particle/object

F_m = ma qvb = m\frac{v^2}{r} qb = m\frac{v^2}{r} qbr = mv^2 \frac{qbr}{m} = v^2 \sqrt{\frac{qbr}{m}} = v
