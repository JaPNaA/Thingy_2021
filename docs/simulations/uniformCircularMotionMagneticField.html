The Math

The force from the magnetic field can be represeted by:

\vec F_m = q\vec v \times \vec B

Because we know that the object is moving in a uniform circle and the magnetic field is always pointing out of the screen, we don't need to worry about the direction.

F_m = qvB

We can use Newton's Second Law to tie the magnetic force and uniform circular motion equations to find the velocity of the particle.

F_m = ma qvB = m\frac{v^2}{r} qB = m\frac{v}{r} qBr = mv \frac{qBr}{m} = v
